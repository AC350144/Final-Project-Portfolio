About Me

Growing up, I was that kid who spent hours gaming and scrolling through the incredible artwork shared by social media creators. It didn't take long before I picked up a sketchpad (and eventually a tablet) to start creating my own pieces. Those early experiments turned into freelance gigs designing artwork for school clubs, which was super fun and gave me a taste of how art could make an impact. Fast forward to college, and I've started exploring how art and tech come together in UI/UX design. I think it's fascinating to see how creative design shapes how people experience technology. When I'm not coding or sketching, you can find me trying out new foods or sampling matcha drinks from different cafes — I love discovering new flavors! I'm also a bit of a gamer, a casual tennis player, and a collector of all things cute and artsy. If it's from Sanrio or Pop Mart, there's a good chance I already own it or want to! These days, I'm diving into the world of UI/UX design, and I'm always excited to learn more about it. If there's one thing I enjoy, it's taking on projects that let me think creatively, build something meaningful, and have a little fun along the way.
